Mock Portfolio


a stock research application written with pure JavaScript and Chart.js that shows users how tech stocks have changed over the last 6 months. Users can adjust the number of shares in any of the companies listed and the line graph and pie chart will update, showing you how your stocks have faired over time.

The stock list fixed to the top of the screen also hase an automatic price updating feature. During trading hours the stock prices listed on the fixed section on the top of the page will update. The ticker will change from red to green depending on if the stock is negative or positive for the trading day.
